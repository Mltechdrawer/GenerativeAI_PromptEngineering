    Generative AI + Prompt Engineering

    An 3-hour course focused on the practical use of Generative AI and Prompt Engineering.

    Welcome to the Generative AI + Prompt Engineering course, where you will explore how modern AI systems are transforming the way we work, learn, and create. Throughout this course, you will develop a clear understanding of the capabilities and limitations of generative AI across text, image, video, and code. You will learn how to select the most appropriate tools depending on your objective, whether it is productivity, research, or content creation. In addition, you will design structured prompts by incorporating elements such as role, context, tasks, constraints, and format, improving them iteratively. Finally, you will apply these skills to build a personalized AI assistant for a real-world use case in areas such as education, business, or software development.
