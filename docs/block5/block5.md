# B V — Practical Applications 

## Final Exercise: Design Your AI Assistant

## Objective

The goal of this activity is to apply the concepts learned during the course by designing a **useful AI assistant for a real-world scenario**.

Students will combine:
- Prompt Engineering  
- Context Engineering  
- Critical thinking  

---

## Task

Design an AI assistant that solves a **real problem** in one of the following contexts (or your own):

- Education  
- Business  
- Software development  
- Personal productivity  
- Research  

---

## Requirements

Your proposal must include:

### 1. Use case
- What problem does your assistant solve?  
- Who is the target user?  

---

### 2. Prompt design  
Write a **well-structured prompt** including:  

- Role  
- Context  
- Task  
- Constraints (if applicable)  
- Output format  

---

### 3. Context design  

Explain:  

- What information the model needs  
- Why that context is relevant  
- What would happen without it  

---

### 4. Create prompt variants

- A simplified version
- An improved/optimized version

---

### 5. Generate an example output

- A simplified version
- An improved/optimized version

---

### 6. Perform a critical evaluation

- What works well?
- What are the limitations?
- How could it be improved?

## Presentation

- Duration: ** 8-10 minutes per group**  
- Explain your assistant clearly and concisely  
- Show your prompt and expected output  

Presentations should include:

- Description of the use case
- Prompt design and structure
- Example output
- Key insights and lessons learned

---

## Evaluation criteria

- **Clarity of the use case**  
- **Quality of the prompt design**  
- **Use of context**  
- **Creativity and usefulness**  
- **Clarity of presentation**  

---

## Tips

- Keep it simple but meaningful  
- Focus on solving a real problem  
- Avoid vague prompts  
- Think about how to improve the output  

---

## Key idea

> A good AI assistant is not defined by the tool,  
> but by how well the problem, prompt and context are designed.
